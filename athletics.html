Athletics

  • Caption One.
  • Caption Two.
  • Caption Three.

Just as the Naval Academy promotes the professional and intellectual development of Midshipmen, so also must it fulfill its responsibility for each Midshipmen's physical development. This is met through a sports program that is one of the broadest in the nation.

In addition to Club and Varsity Sports programs USNA also offers a number of student run Intramural Sports. Further information about USNA's commitment to Physical readiness is available through the Physical Education Department.
