Word-Class Faculty preparing midshipmen

Our faculty is an integrated group of nearly 600 officers and civilians in roughly equal numbers. A cadre of officer faculty with doctorates adds another dimension to the teaching staff as Permanent Military Professors. All career civilian faculty members have doctoral degrees, and many of them are leading scholars in their fields. Working together, our military and civilian instructors form one of the strongest and most dedicated teaching faculties of any college or university in the United States.
